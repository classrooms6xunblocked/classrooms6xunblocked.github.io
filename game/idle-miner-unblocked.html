Idle Miner Unblocked: Built in a Student Apartment, Downloaded 150 Million Times

Kolibri Games made Idle Miner Unblocked in eight weeks from a student apartment and released it July 1, 2016. It hit 150 million downloads by 2022. The concept is classic idle: tap miners, earn cash, hire managers to automate, watch the numbers grow while you do other things. Came back three sessions testing this page. Upgraded the elevator twice during a five-minute break without planning to. That says enough about the loop.

Mine, Automate, Expand

You start with a coal mine. Tap on mineshaft workers to send them mining. They bring ore to the elevator. The elevator carries it to the warehouse. The warehouse converts it to cash. Once you can afford a manager for any of these three stations, that station runs automatically. Your job becomes managing which upgrades to buy next and when to open new mineshaft levels. Eventually the whole mine runs itself and you expand to new resource types and new mines entirely.

The Prestige System

Once your mine is developed enough, you can prestige. This resets your current mine but gives you Super Cash, a premium currency that provides permanent multipliers to all future runs. Your next playthrough earns significantly faster. The idle genre is built on this loop and Idle Miner executes it well. Most players prestige 3 to 5 times before switching mines, each run faster than the last.

Controls

  • Click miners: Send them to work manually before automation
  • Click upgrades: Improve mineshaft, elevator, and warehouse speed
  • Hire managers: Automate each station so it runs without clicking
  • Collect offline earnings: The game earns even when the tab is closed

How to Progress Faster

  • Managers first: The moment you can afford a manager for any station, buy it. Automation compounds. A managed mineshaft plus a managed elevator earns exponentially more than either alone.
  • Elevator is the bottleneck: Most players upgrade mineshafts obsessively and neglect the elevator. If your elevator is slower than your miners, you're losing output. Upgrade elevator speed regularly.
  • Collect offline earnings on return: Each time you open the game, collected offline cash compounds from your last upgrade state. Spend it on upgrades before closing again to maximize the next offline period.
  • Open new shafts early: New mineshaft levels unlock new resources with higher base values. Opening them as soon as possible beats over-upgrading a single shaft.
  • Prestige when growth slows: When upgrades start costing 10x more and producing 2x less, prestige. The Super Cash multiplier from prestige outpaces any amount of grinding at diminishing returns.

Developer History

Idle Miner Tycoon was created by Kolibri Games (originally Fluffy Fairy Games), founded in 2016 by five students in Berlin. Released July 1, 2016 after eight weeks of development. Ubisoft acquired a 75% stake in 2020 at a valuation of €160 million. The studio has expanded the franchise with Idle Restaurant Tycoon and Idle Taxi Tycoon. The browser version runs in HTML5 without plugins.
