Smash Karts Unblocked: Mario Kart Energy in a Browser Tab

If someone told you Mario Kart's battle mode runs in a browser with real players, you'd be skeptical. Smash Karts Unblocked is exactly that. Three-minute rounds, live lobbies, weapons dropping from loot boxes, karts flying everywhere. Spent 40 minutes on this testing the page and forgot I was supposed to be working. No exaggeration. Over 1 million upvotes on Poki alone. The numbers make sense once you play it.

Three Minutes. Real Players. Organized Chaos.

Each match runs exactly three minutes. You drive around an arena, collect weapons from loot boxes, and try to eliminate as many opponents as possible before the timer ends. Weapons include rockets, machine guns, mines, grenades, and an invincibility star that makes you briefly unstoppable. No laps, no finish line. Just kill count. The player with the most eliminations wins. Simple enough to understand in 30 seconds, competitive enough to care about every match.

Your Kart, Your Build

Smash Karts has a full customization system. Driver head, kart body, hat, and celebration animation. All cosmetic, none affect gameplay. But the seasonal content keeps rotating. By June 2026 the game was on Season 18. New hats, new arena hazards, new game modes added regularly. The Arms Race mode introduced in 2025 cycles you through different weapon tiers as you score eliminations. Each weapon plays differently and learning the best use for each one is where the actual skill gap opens up.

Controls

  • W / ↑: Accelerate
  • S / ↓: Brake / reverse
  • A / D or ← →: Steer
  • Space: Use current weapon
  • Drive into loot boxes to pick up weapons automatically

Game Modes Worth Knowing

Free For All is the standard mode. Everyone vs everyone, highest kill count wins. But Smash Karts has rotated in several others over its seasons. Gem Collector has you gathering gems around the arena, but getting hit drops your stash. Candy Rush works similarly with candy instead of gems. Kart Krusher gives one player a power role. Collect keys to become the Krusher, then smash into opponents to score. Score Target is team-based. The variety keeps matches from feeling repetitive even after 20 sessions.

Getting Your First Win

  • Stay moving: A stationary kart is an easy target. Keep circling the arena even when you have a weapon ready. Stopping to aim gets you hit from behind.
  • Rockets at close range: Rockets are the most common weapon early. Fire them when you're close. Long-range rocket shots are easy to dodge by any experienced player.
  • Mines near loot boxes: Drop mines near the loot box spawns. Players driving to pick up weapons aren't watching the ground.
  • Invincibility star, charge in: When you get the star, drive straight at the most crowded part of the map immediately. It lasts seconds. Use every one of them.
  • Arms Race: finish fast: In Arms Race mode, each elimination unlocks your next weapon tier. Prioritize kills over dodging. The player who advances through tiers fastest wins.

Who Built It

Smash Karts was created and published by Tall Team Limited, released in 2020. Built in Unity to run in a standard browser without plugins or downloads.
