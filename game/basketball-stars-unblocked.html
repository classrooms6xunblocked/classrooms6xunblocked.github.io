Basketball Stars Unblocked: NBA Jam Energy in a Browser Tab

MadPuffers made Moto X3M. They also made Basketball Stars Unblocked. Same studio, completely different game. Fast 1v1 basketball matches, big-head caricature players, super shots that can't be blocked when activated. Each match runs under two minutes. Tested it solo against the CPU across several sessions and ended up in tournament mode without planning to. With someone sitting next to you on the same keyboard it gets properly competitive.

Fast Matches, Real Decisions

Basketball Stars is 1v1 or 2v2 arcade basketball. Move, jump, shoot, steal, block. A super shot bar fills as you score. Once full, trigger an unstoppable dunk from anywhere on the court. No defending it. Matches end on timer or score. The stealing mechanic is where the real skill gap opens up. Time a steal right when the ball hits the floor on a dribble and you can take possession before the opponent reacts. Get good at steals and the game opens up.

Solo vs 2-Player

Solo mode puts you against AI in quick matches or tournament mode. The CPU isn't a pushover past the first few rounds. 2-player mode splits the keyboard. Player 1 uses arrow keys, Player 2 uses WASD. Controls

  • Player 1 (← →): Move left and right
  • Player 1 (↑): Jump and shoot
  • Player 1 (↓): Pump fake / block
  • Player 1 (X): Steal the ball
  • Player 1 (Z): Super shot (when bar is full)
  • Player 2 (WASD): Move, jump, pump fake
  • Player 2 (B): Steal
  • Player 2 (V): Super shot

The Super Shot Changes Everything

The super shot bar at the top fills as you score points and make plays. Once full, press your super shot key and your player launches an automatic dunk from anywhere on the court that cannot be blocked. This is the comeback mechanic. Down by two with a full bar means one super shot ties it. Saving the bar for the right moment versus spending it immediately is the main strategic decision in every match. Most beginners spend it too early. Better players hold it for high-pressure moments near the end.

Getting Past the CPU

  • Steal timing is everything: Steal right when the ball bounces on a dribble, not while it's in the air. Mid-air steal attempts almost always miss. Wait for the bounce.
  • Pump fake first against aggressive defenders: Tap ↓ to fake a shot. If the CPU jumps for the block, drive past them while they're in the air. Works consistently until higher difficulty.
  • Save your super shot: Don't use the super shot bar the moment it fills. Wait until you're tied or one point down in the last 30 seconds. One guaranteed basket at the right moment wins matches.
  • Stay under the basket: Most of your shots should come from close range. Long-range shots have lower success rates. Get close, jump, shoot.
  • Tournament mode for practice: Tournament mode pushes the CPU difficulty progressively. The matches toward the final rounds are actually hard and train you faster than quick match.

Developer Background

Basketball Stars was created by MadPuffers, the same studio behind Moto X3M, and released in May 2019. MadPuffers is known for browser-first sports and arcade games that run clean on any device. The game runs in HTML5 directly in Chrome with no downloads or plugins needed.
