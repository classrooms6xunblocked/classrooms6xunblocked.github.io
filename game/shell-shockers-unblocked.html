You're an Egg. You Have a Gun. Go.

Shell Shockers is a 3D browser FPS. You spawn into a map as a weaponized egg, find opponents, and eliminate them before they eliminate you. Seven weapon classes are available: the EggK-47 assault rifle, Scrambler shotgun, Free Ranger sniper, RPEGG rocket launcher, Whipper SMG, Crackshot crossbow, and Tri-Hard three-shot pistol. Each plays differently. Matches run across multiple modes in maps that rotate regularly. The seasonal updates Blue Wizard Digital runs keep the cosmetics and map pool fresh.

Four Game Modes Worth Knowing

Free For All is every egg for themselves. Teams divides the lobby into two sides. Captula the Spatula is capture-the-flag with a spatula as the objective. King of the Coop is king-of-the-hill. Each mode plays differently enough that switching between them keeps sessions from feeling repetitive. Free For All is the best starting point since you're only responsible for yourself. Teams rewards players who actually communicate or coordinate positions.

How to Stop Getting Eliminated Immediately

  • Keep moving: A stationary egg is an easy target. Strafe constantly while aiming. Standing still to shoot accurately costs you more lives than it earns kills.
  • EggK-47 to start: The assault rifle is the most forgiving weapon for new players. Consistent damage at all ranges, manageable recoil. Master it before switching to specialist weapons.
  • Reload behind cover: The reload animation leaves you completely vulnerable. Never reload in the open. Get behind something first.
  • Jump fights: Jumping makes you significantly harder to hit. Jump-strafing while shooting is the difference between intermediate and experienced players.
  • Map awareness over tunnel vision: Most deaths come from opponents approaching from unexpected angles. Keep your camera moving. Never focus so hard on one target that you miss three others approaching.

Blue Wizard Digital

Shell Shockers was created by Blue Wizard Digital, a Canadian independent studio founded by Jason Kapalka, one of the original creators of Bejeweled and Peggle at PopCap. Released September 1, 2017. Built using the BabylonJS WebGL framework, which is why it runs full 3D in a browser without any plugins. Blue Wizard has kept the game updated with seasonal events and new content consistently since launch.
