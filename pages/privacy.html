Privacy Policy

Short version: this site doesn't collect your data, doesn't track you, and doesn't ask for anything personal. You open the site, play a game, close the tab. Nothing is stored on our end.

What We Collect

Nothing that identifies you. No names, no emails, no IP addresses saved to any database on our side. We don't have user accounts, so there's nothing to collect. If you visit and leave, we have no record of it.

Cookies

We don't run advertising cookies or tracking pixels. The site may use minimal browser storage to remember basic preferences like your last selected game category. That data stays in your own browser — it never leaves your device.

Third-Party Games

Games on this site link out to third-party platforms when you click Play Now. Once you leave this site, their own privacy policies apply. Worth checking those separately if you're spending a lot of time on a specific game.

Kids and Students

This site is designed to be safe for all ages. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone — children or adults. There's nothing to fill in and nothing to submit, so there's nothing to collect.

Questions

If something here doesn't make sense or you have a specific concern, the contact page is the right place to reach us. We'll reply within a couple of days.
