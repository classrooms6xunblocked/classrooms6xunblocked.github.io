About Classroom 6x

Built for students. Powered by a love of gaming. Free — always.

Our Mission

Classroom 6x was created with one goal: give students a fast, clean, and completely free place to play games during their free time — without battling school network blocks or dealing with cluttered, ad-heavy sites.

Every game in our library is carefully selected to work instantly in the browser. No downloads. No installs. No accounts. Just open the site and play — on any device, including school Chromebooks.

We believe a good gaming break actually helps students focus better. Short sessions between classes reduce stress and improve concentration. Classroom 6x makes those breaks possible, wherever you are.

What We Offer

A growing library of 100+ hand-picked games across every genre — action, racing, puzzle, multiplayer, sports, and idle. New games added regularly. Fully mobile optimized. Zero personal data collected. Zero cost.
