About Classroom 6x Unblocked

Built for students. No bloat, no restrictions. Just games that work.

Why This Site Exists

Most gaming sites stop working the moment you open them on a school network. You click a game, get a block page, try another site, same result. Classroom 6x Unblocked was built to fix that — not with workarounds or proxies, but with HTML5 games that run as clean browser code, indistinguishable from any other webpage.

No downloads. No plugins. No account. Open a tab, pick a game, play. Close the tab when you're done and nothing is left behind — no history on the device, no saved data, nothing. That was the whole idea from day one.

What Gets Added to the Library

Every game gets tested before it goes up. If it loads slow, has broken controls, or crashes on a Chromebook — it doesn't make the cut. The library stays focused on games that actually run well on school hardware, because that's the whole point.

New games get added when something worth adding shows up. Not on a schedule, not to hit a number — just when a game is genuinely good and works the way it should.

How to Get in Touch

Game requests, bug reports, DMCA notices — the contact page handles all of it. Response time is usually within a couple of days.
