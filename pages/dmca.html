DMCA Policy

Classroom 6x Unblocked doesn't host game files on its own servers. Games here link to third-party platforms — we're essentially a directory, not a file host. That said, if something on this site is causing a copyright problem for you, reach out and we'll sort it out quickly.

How to Send a Removal Request

Send us the following through the contact page:

The URL on this site where the content appears. A description of the copyrighted work you believe is being infringed. Your name and contact email. A short statement that you believe the use is unauthorized.

That's it. We don't need legal threats or formal notices to act — a clear message is enough. We'll respond within 48 hours and remove the content if the claim is valid.

What Happens After You Report

We review every report manually. If the claim checks out, the content comes down the same day. If we need more information, we'll reply and ask for it directly. We're not trying to protect infringing content — we just want to make sure removals are based on real issues.
